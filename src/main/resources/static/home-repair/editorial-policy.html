Editorial Policy

Our commitment to accuracy, transparency, and editorial independence.

Last Updated: January 2026

Core Editorial Principles

1. Evidence-Based Analysis Only

Every cost estimate, risk assessment, and repair recommendation must be traceable to a verifiable data source:

  • Primary sources: RSMeans¬Æ Construction Cost Data, BLS wage statistics, ASHRAE climate data
  • Secondary sources: Peer-reviewed construction research, ICC building code archives
  • Prohibited sources: Anonymous contractor quotes, unverified user testimonials, opinion blogs

2. Algorithmic Transparency

Our cost calculation methodology is fully documented on the Methodology page. We do NOT use proprietary "black box" algorithms that hide how estimates are derived. Any user should be able to manually reproduce our calculations given the same input data.

3. Zero Tolerance for Conflicts of Interest

We accept NO financial compensation from:

  • Contractors or construction companies seeking preferential recommendations
  • Material suppliers attempting to bias product selections
  • Home inspection services offering referral fees
  • Real estate platforms conditioning data access on favorable outcomes

Our business model is advertising-based (display ads only), ensuring complete editorial independence.

4. Conservative Risk Flagging

When dealing with safety-critical issues (electrical hazards, structural defects, hazardous materials), we intentionally err on the side of caution. It is better to over-warn users about potential Chinese drywall or polybutylene plumbing than to minimize legitimate risks.

Corrections Policy

If you identify a factual error, data inaccuracy, or calculation mistake:

  1. Report it: Email spainhyeok@gmail.com with specific details and supporting documentation
  2. Review timeline: We investigate all correction requests within 5 business days
  3. Transparency: If an error is confirmed, we update the affected content and publish a correction notice stating what was changed and when

Important Disclaimer

This platform provides informational estimates only. It does NOT replace:

  • Professional home inspections by licensed inspectors
  • Detailed contractor quotes for actual job sites
  • Engineering assessments for structural issues
  • Legal advice regarding building code compliance or liability
