2026 HOME REPAIR VERDICT ENGINE: TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION (VER 2026.01)

[1] DATA SOURCE MAPPING

LCI (Location Cost Index): 2026 US Metro Master Data.json

Fields: labor_mult, mat_logistics, mob_fee, disp_tax

UCL (Unit Cost Library): 2026 Integrated Construction & Renovation Cost Library.json

Fields: material_cost_range, labor_hours_per_unit, base_labor_rate_national

PSZ (Property Standards): 2026 US Metro Master Data.json

Fields: avg_house, avg_lot, foundation

RFY (Risk Factors by Year): risk_factors_by_year.json

Fields: eras, critical_risks, remedy_cost_factor

[2] SCALE AUTO-ESTIMATION LOGIC If user input is null, use the following formulas:

Roofing (SQUARE): (avg_house * 1.15) / 100

Interior Floor/Paint (SQFT): avg_house / 1.5

Exterior/Yard (SQFT): avg_lot - avg_house

HVAC (UNIT): avg_house / 500 (1 Ton per 500sqft)

[3] MASTER CALCULATION FORMULA Total_Cost = Mob_Final + sum(Item_Cost * Qty) + RFY_Premium

A. Item_Cost Calculation:

Material: avg(UCL.low, UCL.high) * LCI.mat_logistics

Labor: (UCL.labor_hours * UCL.base_rate * LCI.labor_mult)

Era Adjustment: Labor * RFY.remedy_cost_factor (if applicable)

B. RFY_Premium (Risk Add-ons):

Severity "CRITICAL": Multiply Item_Total by 1.3 (+30%)

Removal Cost "HIGH": Add flat $2,800

Inspection Mandatory: Add flat $650

C. Mob_Final (Mobilization):

Max(UCL.mobilization_base_fee, LCI.mob_fee)

[4] VERDICT & PRIORITY RULES A. Priority Order:

SAFETY: Items with severity "CRITICAL" (e.g., Knob & Tube, Lead Paint)

STRUCTURAL: Roofing, Sewer Lines (Note: Cast Iron lifespan ends 2025)

MECHANICAL: HVAC, Electrical Panel

COSMETIC: Flooring, Painting, Cabinets

B. Verdict Tiers:

APPROVED: Budget >= Total_Cost. Message: "Healthy budget reflecting 2026 rates and risks."

WARNING: Total_Cost * 0.9 <= Budget < Total_Cost. Message: "Risk of low-quality materials or missing abatement fees."

DENIED: Budget < Total_Cost * 0.9. Message: "Insufficient budget for standard local scale."

[5] KEYWORD MAPPING FOR AI CODING

"avg_house_sqft" -> use "avg_house" from Master Data

"labor_multiplier" -> use "labor_mult" from Master Data

"material_logistics" -> use "mat_logistics" from Master Data
